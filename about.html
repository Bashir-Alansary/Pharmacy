About Us

Our Pharmacies is recognized as Iraq’s uncontested leading provider of health and personal care products. A success story that began with one pharmacy established in 1975, the group is now a major corporation driving the country’s healthcare sector . With nearly 40 years of experience serving the Iraqian community, El Safwa Pharmacies has an ambitious plan to cement its leadership across the region. With the belief that success comes from the inside out, we invested in a top-notch development center that nurtures the talents of our team. Highly qualified professionals train our pharmacists and employees
